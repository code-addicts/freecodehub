About HTML / CSS:

1. Company Profile and Brand:

HTML/CSS is a leading platform that provides computer science resources and coding challenges for programmers and technology enthusiasts, along with
interview and exam preparations for upcoming aspirants. With a strong emphasis on enhancing coding skills and knowledge, it has become a trusts destiny
for over 12 million plus registered users worldwide. The platform offers a vast collection of tutorial, practice problem, interview tutorials, article, and courses,
covering various domains of computer science.

Our exceptional mentors hailing from top colleges & organizations have the ability to guide you on a journey from the humble beginnings of coding to the
pinnacle of expertise. Under their guidance watch your skills flourish as we lay the foundation and help you conquer the world of coding.

Our brand is built on the pillars of expertise, accessibility, and community. We strive to empower individuals to enhance their programming skills, to bridges
the gap between academia and industry, and provide a supportive community to the learners. HTML/CSS is committed to promoting technology advanced
and providing opportunities for growth in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

2. Corporate History, Mission, Vision, and Motto:

Corporate History:

HTML/CSS was founded in 2008 by Pawan Suthar with a vision to establish comprehensive platform for computer science education and skill development.
Over the years, the platform has experienced exponential growths, cementing it's position as one of the most trusted renowned names in the programming
community.

Mission:

Our mission is to empower programmers and technology enthusiasts worldwide to excel in their coding skills and unleash their full potential. We wants to a
bridge the gap between theory and practice, equipping individuals with skills and expertise require to tackle real-world challenges in the ever evolving field
of technology and get them prepared for their dream jobs.

Vision:

We envision a world where every programmer has unfettered access to top-tier learning resources, enabling them to continuously enhanced their skills and
flourish amidst the ever-evolving technology landscape. HTML/CSS aspires to be the definitive platform for programmers, empowering them to stay at the
forefront of their careers and make a significant impact in the dynamic tech industry. With the time we have evolved and introduced other core fields learns
courses to support the young aspirants

Motto:

“Learn, Practice, and Exellence” – This motto encapsulates our unwavering dedication to continues learning, hands-on practice, and the pursuit of excellence.
We firmly believe that learning is an ongoing journey that spans a lifetime, and with persistent practice and unwavering dedication, individuals can true excel
in the vast realm of computer science.

3. Company Founders/Directors:

Our founder Pawan Suthar is a visionary entrepreneur and esteemed computer science expert. Fuel by his unwavering passion for coding and education, laid
the very bedrock upon which HTML/CSS stands today and his indomitable spirit has been instrumental in its remarkable growth and resounding success. As
the steadfast driving force behind the company, Pawan remain a beacon of guidance and inspiration, propelling the team to constantly challenging limit and

Our CTO, Shikhar Goel has an impeccable track record of developing revolutionary products, with their innovative solutions serving as a vital catalysts for the
